## 🎨 New Design System

### Color Palette: Royal Purple & Gold
- **Primary:** #6B4CE6 (Royal Purple) - Luxury & Elegance
- **Secondary:** #FFD700 (Gold) - Premium & Wealth  
- **Background:** #F8F9FD (Light Blue-Gray)
- **Surface:** #FFFFFF (Pure White)
- **Text:** #1A1A2E (Almost Black)

### Design Principles
1. **Premium & Luxurious** - Purple & Gold combination
2. **Modern & Clean** - Generous white space
3. **Smooth Animations** - Delightful interactions
4. **Consistent** - Same patterns across all screens
5. **Accessible** - High contrast, large touch targets
